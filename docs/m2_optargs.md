## Syntax

`function functionname(|arg` \[`, arg` \[`,` ...\]\]`) {c -(}`
... `{c )-}`

`function functionname(arg, |arg` \[`,` ...\]`) {c -(}`
... `{c )-}`

`function functionname(arg, arg , |`...`) {c -(}` ...
`{c )-}`

The vertical (or) bar separates required arguments from optional
arguments in function declarations. The bar may appear at most once.
