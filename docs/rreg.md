### Optimization

`optimization_options`: `iterate(#)`, `tolerance(#)`, \[

\]

`g`. `iterate()` specifies the maximum number of iterations; iterations
stop when the maximum change in weights drops below `tolerance()`; and
`log`/`nolog` specifies whether to show the iteration log. These options
are seldom used.

`graph` allows you to graphically watch the convergence of the iterative
technique. The weights obtained from the most recent round of estimation
are graphed against the weights obtained from the previous round.

The following option is available with `rreg` but is not shown in the
dialog box:

`coeflegend`; see
[<strong>[R] estimation options</strong>](estimation%20options##coeflegend).
