## Syntax

Stata for Windows

`cd`

`cd` \[`"`\]`directory_name`\[`"`\]

`cd` \[`"`\]`drive:`\[`"`\]

`cd` \[`"`\]`drive:directory_name`\[`"`\]

`pwd`

Stata for Mac and Stata for Unix

`cd`

`cd` \[`"`\]`directory_name`\[`"`\]

`pwd`

If your `directory_name` contains embedded spaces, remember to enclose
it in double quotes.
