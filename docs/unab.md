## Syntax

Expand and unabbreviate standard variable lists

`unab lmacname :`
\[[varlist](http://www.stata.com/help.cgi?varlist)\]
\[`, min(#) max(#) name(string)`\]

Expand and unabbreviate variable lists that may contain time-series
operators

`tsunab lmacname :`
\[[varlist](http://www.stata.com/help.cgi?varlist)\]
\[`, min(#) max(#) name(string)`\]

Expand and unabbreviate variable lists that may contain time-series
operators or factor variables

`fvunab lmacname :`
\[[varlist](http://www.stata.com/help.cgi?varlist)\]
\[`, min(#) max(#) name(string)`\]
