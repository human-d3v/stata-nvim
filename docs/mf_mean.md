`real rowvector mean(X` \[`, w`\]`)`

`real matrix`<span class="nowrap"> _ `variance(X` \[`, w`\]`)`

`w`\]`)`

`real matrix`<span class="nowrap"> _ `correlation(X` \[`,`
`w`\]`)`

`real matrix`<span class="nowrap"> _ `quadcorrelation(X` \[`,`

`w`: `real colvector w` and is optional.
