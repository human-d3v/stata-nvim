## Syntax

Compute sample size

`power twomeans m1 m2` \[`, power(numlist) options`\]

Compute power

`power twomeans m1 m2, n(numlist)` \[`options`\]

Compute effect size and experimental-group mean

`power twomeans m1, n(numlist) power(numlist)` \[`options`\]

where `m1` is the mean of the control (reference) group and `m2` is the
mean of the experimental (comparison) group. `m1` and `m2` may each be
specified either as one number or as a list of values in parentheses
(see
[<strong>numlist</strong>](http://www.stata.com/help.cgi?numlist)).
